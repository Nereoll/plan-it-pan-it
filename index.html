Welcome to Plan it & Pan it

Welcome to Plan it & Pan it – your all-in-one meal planning and recipe discovery app! With Plan it & Pan it, you simply enter the ingredients in your fridge, and our smart system uses TheMealDB API to generate delicious recipe options tailored to your pantry. Whether you're planning meals for the week or looking to try something new, our app helps you filter, save, and manage your favorite recipes directly in your browser. Enjoy a seamless experience with no duplicate favorites, a searchable favorites list, and an intuitive interface that makes meal planning fun and effortless. Dive into your culinary adventure today and let your ingredients inspire your next great meal!
